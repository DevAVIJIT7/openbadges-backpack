What is the backpack

Store the Open Badges you earn and share them between platforms, anywhere on the web. It gives you total control over your own achievements by allowing you to organise and share your badges with whomever you want, wherever you want.

As you earn badges across different platforms and providers, the backpack is a lifelong, central store that means your badges will be safe and accessible, even if you lose access to those issuing platforms and providers.

It's simple to use, just setup a free account and upload or push the badges you have earned into it. The backpack is trusted worldwide, with over 1 million badges already stored.

Find out more at openbadges.org and join the conversation here.
